Get involved with Words & pictures

Do you want to sell your comics at WORDS AND PICTURES 2020? Or are you interested in getting a better idea of what it takes to run an event like this? Or maybe you work for an organisation that has stacks of cash to spare and you want to put it towards the arts.

Well if any of the above applies to you then you've come to the right place! Read on below to find out how you can get involved!

Exhibit your comics

Are you an independent comic book creator looking to exhibit your work at the North-East's largest comic book gathering?

The we have good news!

We're now taking bookings for stalls at the event. If you would like a table, please contact wordsandpictures@wpmail.com with the sbject '2020 Exhibitor' by 30 August 2020.

Volunteer

Every year we recruit a huge team of volunteers without whom, the event just could not run. Volunteers are an essential part of the event, providing knowledge to our visitors.

But it's not just about helping us, this is a fantastic opportunity with a number of benefits:

  • Free entry to the convention
  • Priority access to the Mid-con Party @ By the River Brew
  • An exclusive volunteer t-shirt designed by Rachel Smythe and signed by our six headlining guests
  • £20 expenses per day to cover travel and food
  • You get to pick one comics creator attending the event and have a candle lit dinner with them

Sponsor the event
