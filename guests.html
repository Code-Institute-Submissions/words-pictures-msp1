Kelly Thompson

Kelly Thompson is a writer of novels and comics and formerly a comics critic and creator of the CBR column She Has No Head! Perhaps best known for writing Jem and The Holograms from IDW, and co-writing Marve's A-Force and Captain Marvel & The Carol Corps, as well as her Dark Horse graphic novel Heart In A Box, she also wrote the superhero prose novel The Girl Who Would Be King.

Kyle Higgins

Kyle Higgins (born June 12, 1985) is an American comic book writer and film director. He is best known for his work on the Batman franchise at DC Comics and the Mighty Morphin Power Rangers franchise at Boom! Studios.

Jen Bartel

Jen Bartel is an Eisner award-winning illustrator and comic artist who is best known for her ongoing cover work for clients like Marvel, Disney, and Chronicle Books. She has also collaborated with brands like Adidas, Red Bull, and Aussie. Jen is the co-creator and artist of “BLACKBIRD” from Image Comics.

Rachel Smythe

Rachel Smythe is the creator of webcomic The Doctor Pepper Show and the ongoing webtoon series Lore Olympus. Her work has also appeared in the New Zealand anthology New Ground.

Gene Luen Yang

Gene Luen Yang is a graphic novelist and cartoonist whose work for young adults demonstrates the potential of comics to broaden our understanding of diverse cultures and people. ... In American-Born Chinese (2006), Yang integrates tropes from American comics, Chinese folklore, and the Chinese immigrant experience.

Gail Simone

GAIL SIMONE is the multiple award winning writer of WONDER WOMAN, BIRDS OF PREY, DEADPOOL, THE SIMPSONS, and many other classic runs of some of the world's most popular comics. She has also written for animation, prose and video games. Known for her strong character work and dark humor, LEAVING MEGALOPOLIS shows Gail at her most ferocious and untamed.

John Allison

John Allison is the principal creator, writer and artist for the Scary-Go-Round body of work, including Bobbins, Scary Go Round, Bad Machinery and Giant Days as well as several other comics set both in and outside of the Tackleverse.

Jordie Bellaire

Jordie is an American colorist living in Dublin. A few collaborations from 2012--John Carter: Gods of Mars, Hulk: Season One, Doctor Strange: Season One, Manhattan Projects with Jonathan Hickman and Rocketeer Cargo of Doom with Mark Waid & Chris Samnee. Her upcoming projects are Mara with Brian Wood & Ming Doyle, Pretty Deadly with Kelly Sue DeConnick & Emma Rios, and Nowhere Men with Eric Stephenson & Nate Bellegarde

Marguerite Bennett

Marguerite Bennett is an American comic book writer. She has worked on Bombshells, Angela, Josie and the Pussycats, and her creator-owned books InSeXts and Animosity.

Hannah Blumenreich

Hannah Blumenreich is a full time freelancer in Minneapolis, MN. She is typically working on her own comics or trying to figure out if her car's been towed.

Sophie Campbell

Sophie Campbell is the Eisner-nominated creator of the series Wet Moon and Shadoweyes, and is also known for her work on Teenage Mutant Ninja Turtles, Jem & the Holograms, and Glory. She is based in Rochester, New York, and loves ice cream and cats.

Daniele Di Nicuolo

Born and raised in Milano, Daniele Di Nicuolo debuted in American comics with Mirror’s Edge by Dark Horse. Then he started his collaboration with BOOM! Studios, becoming the main artist of the miniseries, Mighty Morphin Power Rangers: Pink. After a collaboration with IDW Publishing on The Infinite Loop Vol. 2, Di Nicuolo reunited with BOOM! Studios as the main artist of the ongoing Mighty Morphin Power Rangers series during the acclaimed Shattered Grid event.

Bilquis Evely

Bilquis Evely is a comics artist based in São Paulo, Brazil who is currently the artist on the new Wonder Woman series from DC Comics. Evely has worked on a number of titles for DC — including Batman, Legends of Tomorrow and the Shadow — as well as titles for for Archie Comics and Dynamite Entertainment.

Ryan Parrott

Ryan Parrott is a television and comic book writer. He served as an assistant to J.J. Abrams on Star Trek Into Darkness, and wrote the Star Trek: Ongoing issue "The Voice of a Falling Star". He subsequently co-wrote IDW Publishing's Star Trek: Starfleet Academy series with writer Mike Johnson.

Dafna Pleban

Dafna Pleban is a comic book editor working for BOOM! Studios. She broke into the industry by interning at Entertainment Weekly before being hired by BOOM! Studios to start and organize their podcast. That led to a position as an editor in 2006, where she began working on their Eureka tie-in comic, Eureka: Dormant Gene. Over her time at the company, she has been the primary editor for their various Planet of the Apes and Sleepy Hollow series.

Nicola Scott

Nicola Scott (born July 27) is a comics artist from Sydney, Australia whose notable works include Birds of Prey and Secret Six. In 2016, she drew Wonder Woman for DC Comics Rebirth. Also in 2016, she and Greg Rucka created a comic series entitled Black Magick, which was published through Image Comics.

Kyle Starks

Kyle Starks. Kyle Starks is the Eisner nominated creator of Sexcastle which was recently optioned to be made into a major motion picture. Whoa! He's also written a bunch of other great stuff like Rick and Morty and Invader Zim and is generally recognized as a pretty good, laugh making, funny boy.

Uru-Chan

Chelsey Han, better known as Uru-chan is the author of unORDINARY . In addition to unORDINARY, she is also responsible for writing OFR Ice (which is currently on hiatus).
