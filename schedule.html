Your Con Schedule

Saturday

Time Location Event
10-11am Event Space 3rd floor
Fan-fiction/Art for the community

Join Hannah Blumenreich, creator of the Spider-man fan comic Spidey-zine along with a panel full of comics' most passionate fan fiction writers as they discuss fan comics, fan fiction and the importance of creating your own work when you feel you aren't been seen.
11am-12pm BALTIC Library
Women in Comics - Comics in stasis!

Join creators Gail Simone, Jen Bartel, Kelly Thompson & Sophie Campbell as they discuss toppling the uninclusive culture and outdated systems that decide who gets what opportunity in the comics industry.
1-2pm BALTIC Library
My Bubbles!!!!

Thought Bubbles? Word Baloons? Speech Bubbles? ....verbal clouds? Join us as we battle it out to decide once and for all what the correct nomenclature really is for comics' primary method of delivering dialogue.

2-3pm BALTIC Square Marquee
Kickstarting a comic with Kyle Starks

It's 2020! All the best comics are the result of a successful Kickstarter now! Kyle Starks - writer and artist of a number of successfully Kickstarted comics such as Old Head, Kill Them All and Sexcastle- discusses what makes a successful Kickstarter.
.....also....back my autobiographical comic From Wannabe Comics Writer to Doing a Web Developer Course in Six Months: An Oddyssey ...You can do that here.
3-4pm BALTIC Square Marquee
Webtoons - Give us a damn Eisner!!!

Webtoons is an online platform that's home to some of the best and most accessible comics being released right now, and it's all Free. But somehow they were still totally snubbed for the Eisner Awards nominations this year. Rachel Smythe creator of Lore Olympus and Uru-Chan creator of unOrdinary have some things to say about this.
4-5pm Event Space 3rd Floor
Power Rangers - Better than the show!

Kyle Higgins. Ryan Parrott. Daniele Di Niculo. Dafna Pleban. All come together for a retrospective on bringing the Power Rangers to a modern comics audience and how they improved on the much loved but frankly pretty terrible 90's show.

Sunday

Time Location Event

Paper Quality - a heated debate about page thickness

Do you like them nice thick pages modern comics use to put up the cover price? Or maybe you're Nostalgic for the good old days of newspaper quality paper. Maybe there's a somewhere in between. We put it to debate. There will be tears.
