This is Words and pictures!

WORDS AND PICTURES is Gateshead's community celebration of comic books and everything around them. It is an event for fans of all types of comics, from the big-two to modern web comics. But it is also an event that supports creators and promotes discussion about diversity and inclusivity within the comics industry. Our biggest hope is that by coming along you don't just feel like you're attending an event, you're also joining a community.
